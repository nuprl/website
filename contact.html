How to find us

440 Huntington Ave,
Boston, MA 0211

The College of Computer and Information Science is
located in Building WVH (aka West Village "H" -- don't forget the H,
otherwise you won't be able to distinguish it from WV "A" through WV "G").
We are diagonally across the street from the Museum of Fine Arts.
It's the only 16-story silver-colored tower on campus -- you can't miss it.

To get to all CCIS offices, take the elevators opposite the big glassed-in lab on the first floor.


The administrative offices and mail boxes are located in Room 202 WVH, to your left as you get off the elevator.


The PRL lab is in room 308 behind the glass wall with Racket code on them.


Talks are often in room 366 is located to your right as you get off the elevator on the third floor.
